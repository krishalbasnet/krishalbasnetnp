  1. Organic Polymers
    • PVC (Polyvinyl chloride)
      • →It is obtained by polymerzation of Vinyl Chloride, under pressure in presence of benzoylperoxide as catalyst.
    • Bakelite
    • Polystyrene
      • →It is obtained by polymerzation of Vinyl Benzene, under pressure in presence of benzoylperoxide as catalyst.
    • Teflon:- Polytetrafluoroethylene,Fluon(PTFE)
      • →It is obtained by polymerization of tetrafluoroethylene, under pressure in presence of benzoylperoxide as catalyst.
    • Epoxy Raisin
      • →It is obtained by polymerization of Bisphenol-A and epichlorohydrin.
    • Polyurethene
      • →It is obtained by polymerization of Hexamethylenediisocyanate and 1,4-Butanediols.
    • Nylon 6,6
      • →It is obtained by condensation polymerization of Adipic acid and Hexamethylenediamines.
