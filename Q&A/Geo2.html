How engineering Geological map prepared?
Engineering geological maps involves :
  1. Scale : Map scale will determine how much information is presented.
  2. Physical & engineering geological properties of rock should be considered
  3. Hydrological Condition : Provision of Hydrological information on map facilities prediction of undesirable change in hydrological regime and recommendation of methods to avoid them.
  4. Geomorphological Condition : Evaluation of Geomorphological conditions is more than a simple description of surface topography. It should provide explanation for relation between surface conditions and geology. It should explain the origin, age and development of individual Geomorphological element<. /li>
  5. Geodynamic Condition : Seismic & Volcanic activity and tectonic movements resulting in uplift or depression of the surface. Mapable features include:
    • Intensity of seismic event using isoseismic line.
    • Surface expression of present day and historical events.
    • Terraces and manmade structures.
