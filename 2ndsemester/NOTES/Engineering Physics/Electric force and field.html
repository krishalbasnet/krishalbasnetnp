  1. Electric charge

    Important Results obtained from experiment by Du Fay & Gray :-

    • The transmission of electric charges to the ground is called "The Earthing", where the Earth is assured as the sea of charge.

    • There are two types of materials on the basis of transmission of charges: which are "conductor" such as metal and "insulator" such as glass, amber, plastics etc.

    • Two types of charges can be classified. It was further confirmed by pith-ball experiments. Such as electric field glass rubbed with fur produced "positive charge" and "negative charge" to that on ebonite rod rubbed with silk.
