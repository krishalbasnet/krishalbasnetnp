10. Electric Force and Field
  • Important results concluded by the experiment of Du Pay and Gray:-
    1. The transmission of electric charges to the ground is called "The Earthing", where the Earth is assured as the sea of charges.
    2. There are two types of materials on the basis of transmission of charges: which are "conductor" such as metal and "insulator" such as glass, amber, plastics etc.
    3. Two types of charges can be classified. It was further confirmed by pith-ball experiments. Such as electric field glass rubbed with fur produced "positive charge" and "negative charge" to that on ebonite rod rubbed with silk.
