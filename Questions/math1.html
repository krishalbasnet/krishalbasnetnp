#. Differentiation under Integral sign
  1. \(\int_0^{\infty} x^ne^{-ax}dx\)
  2. \(\int_0^{\pi} \frac{dx}{a+bcosx}dx \) and deduce \(\int_0^{\pi} \frac{dx}{(a+bcosx)^2}dx \).
  3. \(\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \frac{dx}{(a^2sin^2x+b^2cos^2x)^2}dx \)
  4. \(\int_0^1 \frac{x^\alpha-1}{logx} dx\)
  5. \(\int_0^\infty \frac{tan^{-1}(ax)}{x(1+x^2)}dx \)
  6. \(\int_0^\infty \frac{e^{-x}sinbx}{x}dx \)
  7. \(\int_0^\alpha \frac{log(1+\alpha x)}{1+x^2} dx\) and deduce \(\int_0^1 \frac{log(1+\alpha x)}{1+x^2} dx\)
