Soil Mechanics - Notes


Introduction
Soil Mechanics :
Soil mechanics is the scientific study of the physical, mechanical, and hydraulic properties of soil and its behavior under various conditions. It is a fundamental branch of geotechnical engineering, which deals with the design and construction of structures that interact with the ground.
