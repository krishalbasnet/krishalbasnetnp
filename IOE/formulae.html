Trigonometry
Addition & Substraction
$$(A+B) = sinA cosB + cosA sinB $$ $$(A-B) = sinA cosB - cosA sinB$$ $$(A+B)=cosAcosB-sinAsinB$$ $$(A-B)=cosAcosB+sinAsinB$$
Sum to Product
$$sinA + sinB = 2sin(\frac{A+B}{2}).cos(\frac{A-B}{2})$$ $$sinA - sinB = 2cos(\frac{A+B}{2}).sin(\frac{A-B}{2})$$ $$cosA + cosB = 2cos(\frac{A+B}{2}).cos(\frac{A-B}{2})$$ $$cosA - cosB = -2sin(\frac{A+B}{2}).sin(\frac{A-B}{2})$$
Product to Sum
$$sin A cos B = \frac{1}{2} [ sin (A + B) + sin (A - B) ]$$ $$cos A sin B = \frac{1}{2} [ sin (A + B) - sin (A - B) ]$$ $$cos A cos B = \frac{1}{2} [ cos (A + B) + cos (A - B) ]$$ $$sin A sin B = \frac{1}{2} [ cos (A - B) - cos (A + B) ]$$
